Three things to know.

  • Surgical, not creative — Edit's strongest move is removing or replacing one thing, not reimagining the whole image.
  • Minimal prompts win — say what changes. "Remove X, keep everything else exact." Long prompts confuse the model.
  • Use it as a prep step — strip styling, clean references, isolate subjects before packshots, training, or video. Edit is the first link in the chain.
